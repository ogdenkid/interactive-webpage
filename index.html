Our story

Our story began when Saitama realized that becoming a hero was easy compared to finding the right outfit. Tired of flimsy gloves and suits that couldn’t keep up with serious punches, he started making his own durable red gloves and reliable yellow spandex. What began as one bald hero solving his own problem quickly turned into a shop dedicated to helping bald heroes everywhere suit up and look the part.
